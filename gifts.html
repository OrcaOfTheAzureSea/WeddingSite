Gifts

We’re tying the knot, the big day is near,

To celebrate love with those we hold dear.

If you’re thinking of gifts, we’ll give you a clue—

We’ve got all the towels (and a toaster or two).

No pots or pans, no vases or chairs,

Just help us start life with fewer wares!

A small donation, if you feel inclined,

Would help us make memories, sweet and divine.

But truly, your presence is all that we need,

To laugh, to dance, and to love—indeed!
